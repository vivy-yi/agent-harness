# From Research Question to Scientific Workflow: Leveraging Agentic AI for Science Automation

- **arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21910
- **Published:** 2026-04-25
- **Summary:** Proposes an agentic architecture with three layers (semantic LLM, deterministic DAG generator, knowledge Skills) to auto-convert research questions into scientific workflows. Skills raise intent accuracy from 44% to 83%. LLM overhead <15s, cost <$0.001/query on Kubernetes.
- **Relevance:** Agent harness for scientific automation; good reference for skill-based agent design
