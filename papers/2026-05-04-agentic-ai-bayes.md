# Position: agentic AI orchestration should be Bayes-consistent

## 基本信息
- **论文链接**：http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00742v1
- **发表日期**：2026-05-01
- **分类**：Agent Theory / Bayesian

## 摘要
LLMs excel at predictive tasks and complex reasoning tasks, but many high-value deployments rely on decisions under uncertainty. This position paper argues that agentic AI orchestration should be Bayes-consistent.
