# When LLMs Stop Following Steps: A Diagnostic Study of Procedural Execution in Language Models

## 基本信息
- **论文链接**：http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00817v1
- **发表日期**：2026-05-01
- **分类**：LLM Benchmark / Procedural Reasoning

## 摘要
Large language models (LLMs) often achieve strong performance on reasoning benchmarks, but final-answer accuracy alone does not show whether they fail at procedural execution. This diagnostic study examines when LLMs stop following steps.

## 关键内容
- 诊断LLM程序执行失败原因
- 发现最终答案准确率掩盖了过程失败
- 揭示LLM在步骤执行中的缺陷
- 推理基准测试的局限性
