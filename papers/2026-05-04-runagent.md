# RunAgent: Interpreting Natural-Language Plans with Constraint-Guided Execution

## 基本信息
- **论文链接**：http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00798v1
- **发表日期**：2026-05-01

## 摘要
Humans solve problems by executing targeted plans, yet large language models (LLMs) remain unreliable for structured workflow execution. We propose RunAgent, a multi-agent plan execution platform that interprets natural-language plans with constraint-guided execution.

## 关键内容
